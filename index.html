kiwappPDF.js

Enhance your app with native PDF reader on Android and iOS devices.

Why a native PDF reader ?

Read PDF in HTML5 can be slow and unstable with big files. With the PDF reader, the PDF are opened as if they were opened by native. Really fast.

This library needs Kiwapp.js loaded on your app to work.
